Breakfast Taco

This viral breakfast taco consists of a small tortilla, some jalapenos, cheese, and an egg. Cooking is simple since it all takes place in an airfryer.

Ingredients

  • Small Tortilla
  • Sliced Jalapenos
  • Shredded Cheese
  • One Whole Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 350
  2. Place jalapenos around the edge of the tortilla
  3. Crack an egg into the middle of the tortilla
  4. Top with shredded cheese, salt, and pepper
  5. Cook in the air fryer for atleast 5 minutes, longer if you want the egg cooked more
