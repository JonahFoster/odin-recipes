Pulled Pork

This delicious pulled pork recipe will require patience, but the result is worth it!

Ingredients

  • Pulled Pork
  • BBQ Rub
  • Coke

Steps

  1. Trim excess fat from the pulled pork
  2. Cut pork into four roughyl even pieces
  3. Season all sides thoroughly, patting it into the pork
  4. Place pork into a slow cooker and pour Coke around it.
  5. Let cook for 8 hours on low
  6. Shred with a fork and serve!
