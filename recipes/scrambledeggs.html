Scrambled Eggs

A simple scrambled eggs recipe for a quick breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Whole Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the pan to medium-high heat
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk thoroughly.
  3. Pour eggs into a pan and stir constantly
  4. Towards the end of cooking, season with salt and pepper
  5. After a few minutes, serve hot
